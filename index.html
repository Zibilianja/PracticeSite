Welcome to All Grow Counseling

Jonathan Fuller MA, LPCC

A holistic approach to therapy and wellness aimed to help you grow anew.

There are moments in all of our lives that offer a place to start again, to reflect on our past, to learn and to grow. I am here to help support that process and assist clients to grow into who they want to be, to let go of the weight of their past, and to heal so that they may meet their future with a deeper sense of purpose and empowerment.

I am trained in Mindfulness Based Transpersonal Counseling and utilize methods including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Internal Family Systems, and Gestalt. My practice focuses on your strengths and utilizing your passions to help support your growth and expand your roots.
