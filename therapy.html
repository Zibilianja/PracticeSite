About Therapy

Life is a difficult journey for everyone and each of us have our own strengths and limitations. Often we focus so much on our struggle that we ignore our strengths and forget how to appreciate life.

We seek ways to avoid the discomfort of our daily lives, often by running from the weight of our past or avoiding the uncertainty of the future. There are many ways to escape in this world including food, substances, sex, money to name a few. As we get better at avoiding our discomfort, the feelings we push aside come out in the form of anxiety, depression, addiction or disorder.

My goal is to help you on your path to take the next step towards growth. All Grow focuses on functionality rather than dysfunction. By cultivating your natural wisdom, together we will find ways to work with the obstacles you face in your relationships, career, spiritual path and daily life.

I welcome all spiritual, religious and cultural perspectives. By exploring our perspectives we can better understand the limitations and expectations of our beliefs and find a deeper sense of meaning and purpose in our lives. We will establish real goals, nurturing personal growth and bringing you into relationship with your authentic self.

Methods we might utilize:
Meditation, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Gestalt, Internal Family Systems, Transpersonal, Jungian Dreamwork, Shamanic Counseling, Prayer, Ritual, Relational Therapy, Trauma Work, Art Therapy, Horticultural Therapy
