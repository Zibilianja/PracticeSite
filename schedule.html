I know it can feel intimidating, vulnerable and just in general anxiety inducing to reach out to a therapist. If you want, I offer a free 15 minute consultation and interview in order to see if we are a good fit. More imporant than seeking help from a therapist, is making sure they are right for you. The number one predictor of success in therapy is a client's willingness to engage, closely followed by the relationship between client and therapist. In other words, if you are really dedicated to changing, most therapists should work for you, but if you are like the majority of people, it can be challenging to engage all the time and so the relationship and trust between you and your therapist is of the utmost importance.
